About me


My name is Tjerk Bakker and I live in Alblasserdam. I enjoy gaming, watching movies, and listening to music. I started an education in Software Development, something I had always felt inspired to pursue. However, things didn’t go entirely as planned. While studying, I discovered that it wasn’t really for me. Sitting still for hours, staring at a screen, and typing all day just didn’t suit me. So, I decided to quit and switch to an education in Electrical Engineering, specializing as a First Mechanic. I chose this path because I believe it suits me better, being active throughout the day while still working with technology.
